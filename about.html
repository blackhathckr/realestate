About Us

Your Trusted Real Estate Partner Since 2013

Our Story

Established in 2013, Kartikeya Realty began its journey with a simple yet powerful vision: to bring professionalism, transparency, and trust to the real estate sector. What started as a modest two-member team has now grown into a 24-member strong, full-service property consultancy, serving clients across Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Pune, Bhopal, Indore, and beyond.

Our Experience

Our founders bring with them years of ground-level experience with some of India's most renowned developers. Having worked closely with leading names such as Kolte Patil, Gera Developments, Rohan Builders, Lodha Group, and VTP Realty in Mumbai & Pune, we've gained in-depth knowledge of the dynamics that shape urban real estate markets.

In Delhi NCR, we've had the privilege of being associated with major projects by Omaxe, Cosmic Group, Jaypee Greens, and Ashiana Housing. This exposure across India's key markets has helped us develop a sharp eye for value-driven opportunities and investor-friendly properties.
